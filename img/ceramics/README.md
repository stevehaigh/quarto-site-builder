# Ceramics

Drop your ceramic pieces here as `.jpg` or `.png`.

The page in `personal.qmd` references `placeholder-01.svg` through `placeholder-06.svg`. Replace those filenames in `personal.qmd` with your real image filenames once you've added images.

## Recommended

- **Aspect ratio**: square (1:1) works best in a 3-column grid. ~1200×1200 px is plenty for web.
- **Format**: JPEG, quality ~85. Keep file sizes under ~400 KB per image.
- **Naming**: lowercase, hyphens, descriptive — `stoneware-vessel-2025.jpg`, not `IMG_4837.JPG`.

## Tips

- Shoot against a plain, slightly-warm background (cream linen, raw plywood) — flatters most clay.
- Soft natural light, ideally indirect. North-facing window works.
- For glossy glazes, use a polariser or shoot at a slight angle to avoid hotspots.
- Batch-resize on macOS:
  ```bash
  sips -Z 1200 *.jpg
  ```
